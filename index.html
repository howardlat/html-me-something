My Hobby - Making Candles and Wax Melts

I have had many hobbies throughout my life like crotchet, sewing, and I have even tried painting. After a year or so of doing those things, I became bored and found a new hobby making candles and wax melts.

I enjoy making candles and wax melts because I like the different scents you can add to the wax as well as colors. I also chose to use soy wax to make my candles and melts because it has no chemicals and produces less candle soot which is the dark black ring around the top of the candle container.

Below you will see some of the scented candles and wax melts I have handmade and detailed information about their scents.

Fall Festival Scent

Top notes of sparkling citrus, cinnamon powder and macintosh apple with mid notes of blush rose, clove buds and crushed greens on a base of raspberry jam, scarlet peach and sugared vanilla.

Coconut Lime Verbena Scent

This fragrance is infused with natural essential oils, including coriander, lemon, lime, orange, and tangerine.

Citron and Mandarin Scent

This fragrance oil is infused with natural essential oils, including grapefruit, orange, and lemon oil.

Winter Hideaway Scent

Top notes of frosted mint leaf, coriander, and snow flurries with mid notes of arabian lavender, cedarwood and baltic amber on a base of cypress, wintergreen tree, and smoky vetiver.
